Agency History:

Julio Garcia Insurance Agency was founded by Julio Garcia, Sr in Clute, Texas in 1984, at that time it was an affiliated agency with Farmers Insurance. Julio Sr’s spouse Virginia & daughter Lora both worked in customer service in the agency each working twenty plus years in the agency. In 1998 Julio Garcia, Jr started in the Insurance business, his agency was also affiliated with Farmers Insurance, opening his office in Houston, Texas. In 2008 Julio Jr sold his Houston agency to his brother Darrell Garcia and went to work for Farmers Insurance District offices in McAllen, Texas then Austin, Texas training new agents for Farmers Insurance. In 2003 Julio Sr moved the agency to Lake Jackson, Texas and retired in 2017. Julio Jr took over the reins of the family agency in 2017 and at that time opted to terminate the affiliation with Farmers Insurance and became an Independent Insurance Agency. In 2018 the Agency acquired Best Insurance Agency in Alton, Texas in the Rio Grande Valley. In 2019 Julio Garcia Insurance Agency moved its office to Tomball, Texas and later in 2019 the agency acquired Cruz & Associates Insurance in Houston, Texas.


In the Agencies thirty-five plus year history the agency prides itself on its customer service and customer tenure. The agency is a full service Insurance agency providing both Personal Lines and Commercial Lines Insurance.
